About This Site

This site is intended to provide a place for advocates for better education based on the philosophies of Salman Khan and the efforts of Khan Academy to share problems and ideas and collaborate on efforts to improve educational achievement world-wide.

  • Focus of this site
  • Site Technology
  • Issues/ToDos
  • Developer Help Needed

There are 2 major focuses to this site:

  1. A Discussion Forum where advocates can share and discuss ideas, problems and experiences. Papers, Questions, etc. are divided into 4 broad categories that are represented on the main menu of the site:
    1. Learning Resources: How to obtain or link in Learning resources in each language and education jurisdiction
    2. Learning Platform: How to get a platform available in all applicable languages and provide maximum learning functionality
    3. Learners/Programs: How to get learners involved and succeeding using the platform and learning resources
    4. Misc Discussions: Goals, Strategies, Alliances & Partnerships, Recruitment & Organization, etc.
  2. Translation Efforts related to Khan Academy:
    1. Topic/Learning Unit Names: The names that are visible
    2. Templates: This is the name of the files that have language-related content. Templates fall into the following categories:
      1. Page Content: The templates that contain the static content on pages.
      2. Container Templates: These are the parts of a web page surrounding other templates (currently the site-template and the exercise-wrapper template).
      3. Exercise Templates: These are the templates that contain the actual content for individual exercises.
      4. Function Templates: These are the javascript files that generate content that go into other templates, in particular the Exercise Templates.
      5. Report Templates: These are the templates that contain report content for learners and coaches.
      6. Etc.

    The site is still under development. Some functionalities do not yet exist. Please provide feedback to make the site better and please be patient as we complete and enhance the site.

    NOTE: The translation efforts are based on the philosopy of translating as much as possible as quickly as possible and then tackle the tougher issues. We will find more tougher issues as we proceed, but currently known issues include:

    1. Issues related to right-to-left languages such as Arabic and Hebrew.
    2. Generated text content that involves grammar (especially dealing with langauges where spelling of nouns, verbs, adjectives, pronouns, etc. is based on gender, case, plurality, etc.).
    3. Generated graphic content that might not take language, or methods into consideration.
    4. Issues related to disagreement on desired methods for math and other subjects or on content to be taught.

    Site Technology:

    This site is developed in Python for Google App Engine (because this is the technology being used by Khan Academy). It also uses webapp2, ndb, jQuery (Superfish menu) and ckEditor.
